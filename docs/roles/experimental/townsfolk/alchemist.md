# Alchemist (Experimental)

**Role Type:** Townsfolk  
**Ability:** You have a Minion ability. When using this, the Storyteller may prompt you to choose differently.

---

## 🧠 Overview

Alchemist is a Townsfolk who gains the **ability of a Minion**, but remains good.

You provide:

- access to normally evil abilities
- unusual information or control
- unique strategic interactions

Your power lies in:

- using a Minion ability for good
- understanding how that ability behaves
- leveraging unexpected tools to disrupt evil

---

# 🟢 Playing Alchemist (Good)

## 🎯 Your Role

You are not just a Townsfolk — you are:

> **A good player with an evil ability**

---

## 🟢 Day 1 Priorities

### 1. Understand your ability

Default strong play:

> “What does my ability actually do — and how does it behave for good?”

Why:

- Minion abilities are designed for evil
- their impact may differ when used by good
- you must understand both power and limitations

---

### 2. Decide whether to reveal

You should consider:

- how dangerous your ability is
- whether it provides confirmable value
- whether revealing helps or harms good

---

### 3. Use your ability carefully

You should:

- avoid reckless use
- consider long-term impact
- think about how the Storyteller may guide outcomes

---

## 🧠 Mid-game Strategy

### Use your ability with purpose

Move from:

> “I have a Minion ability.”

to:

> **“How can I use this ability to help good win?”**

---

### Create controlled impact

You can:

- generate information
- disrupt evil plans
- influence game flow

---

### Be aware of interpretation

Your actions may:

- be misread as evil
- create confusion
- require explanation

---

## ⚠️ Common Mistakes (Good)

### ❌ Using the ability blindly

Minion abilities can harm good if misused.

---

### ❌ Revealing without a plan

Information alone is not always helpful.

---

### ❌ Ignoring Storyteller prompts

The Storyteller may adjust outcomes — pay attention.

---

# 😈 Bluffing Alchemist (Evil)

## 🎯 Your Role

As evil, your goal is to:

> **Explain unusual behavior or effects through a “good Minion ability”**

---

## 🟢 Why Alchemist is a strong bluff

- explains strange actions
- accounts for unusual effects
- justifies non-standard behavior
- fits complex games well

---

## 🧩 Core Bluff

> “I’m the Alchemist — I have a Minion ability.”

---

## 🔑 Step 1: Choose a believable ability

### 🟢 Good approach

- pick a Minion ability that fits the game state
- ensure your actions match the claimed ability
- keep it consistent

---

### 🚫 Avoid

- claiming abilities that contradict observed events
- overcomplicating your explanation

---

## 🔑 Step 2: Match behavior to ability

### 🟢 Safe choices

- act consistently with your claimed ability
- provide believable reasoning

---

### 🔴 Risky choices

- inconsistent usage
- unexplained actions

---

## 🔑 Step 3: Control interpretation

You should:

- explain strange outcomes
- justify unexpected interactions
- anchor your behavior in your claim

---

### Example plays

- “That could be from my Alchemist ability.”
- “I’m using a Minion ability — that’s why this looks strange.”

---

## 🔄 Mid-game as evil

### Stay flexible

If the bluff weakens:

- simplify your explanation
- reduce reliance on the ability
- pivot to a normal role if needed

---

### Use your claim strategically

- justify irregular play
- confuse information interpretation
- create ambiguity

---

## ⚠️ Common Mistakes (Evil)

### ❌ Choosing an incompatible ability

Your claim must match observable reality.

---

### ❌ Overusing the ability

Too much complexity makes the bluff fragile.

---

### ❌ Ignoring consistency

Your actions must align with your claim.

---

# 🧠 Advanced Notes

## 🔀 Ability interaction

The Alchemist:

- uses a Minion ability
- remains good
- may be guided by the Storyteller

---

## 🧩 Strategic depth

The value of Alchemist depends heavily on:

- which Minion ability is given
- how it is used
- how well it integrates into the game state

---

## 🧠 Strategic impact

Alchemist shifts the game from:

- **clear team boundaries**

to:

- **mixed ability alignment**

---

## 🧠 Key Insight

> **Alchemist is powerful not because of the ability — but because it breaks expectations about who can have it.**
