# Poppy Grower (Experimental)

**Role Type:** Townsfolk  
**Ability:** Minions & Demons do not know each other. If you die, they learn who each other are that night.

---

## 🧠 Overview

Poppy Grower disrupts the **starting structure of the evil team** by removing their initial coordination.

You provide:

- early-game protection
- coordination disruption
- delayed evil synergy

Your power lies in:

- forcing evil to operate blindly at the start
- increasing the chance of early mistakes
- creating a timing-based shift in game dynamics

---

# 🟢 Playing Poppy Grower (Good)

## 🎯 Your Role

You are not just a Townsfolk — you are:

> **The reason the evil team cannot function properly at the start of the game**

---

## 🟢 Day 1 Priorities

### 1. Stay alive

Default strong play:

> “I don’t need to do anything special — I just need to live.”

Why:

- your ability is strongest while alive
- evil coordination is completely disabled
- every extra day increases good’s advantage

---

### 2. Avoid unnecessary attention

You should:

- avoid risky or flashy claims
- avoid becoming a central target
- blend into normal play

---

### 3. Observe early-game behavior

You should watch for:

- evil players failing to coordinate
- inconsistent or weak evil narratives
- players acting without clear alignment

---

## 🧠 Mid-game Strategy

### Understand the timing shift

Move from:

> “Evil is disorganized.”

to:

> **“Evil may suddenly become coordinated.”**

---

### Prepare for your death

Once you die:

- evil gains full information
- coordination improves dramatically
- the game may accelerate

---

### Use your window

While alive:

- encourage discussion
- allow contradictions to surface
- let evil expose itself through misplays

---

## ⚠️ Common Mistakes (Good)

### ❌ Drawing attention early

You do not need to be active — your ability already is.

---

### ❌ Claiming Poppy Grower too soon

This allows evil to anticipate coordination changes.

---

### ❌ Ignoring the post-death shift

The game changes significantly once you die.

---

# 😈 Bluffing Poppy Grower (Evil)

## 🎯 Your Role

As evil, your goal is to:

> **Explain poor coordination and delay suspicion**

---

## 🟢 Why Poppy Grower is a strong bluff

- explains early confusion
- justifies inconsistent play
- accounts for weak coordination
- fits naturally into many scripts

---

## 🧩 Core Bluff

> “If Poppy Grower is in play, that explains why evil isn’t coordinated.”

---

## 🔑 Step 1: Use it defensively

### 🟢 Good approach

- introduce Poppy Grower when coordination looks weak
- use it to explain mistakes
- normalize disorganized play

---

### 🚫 Avoid

- introducing it too early
- forcing it without visible signs

---

## 🔑 Step 2: Control expectations

### 🟢 Safe choices

- suggest evil will become stronger later
- frame early chaos as expected

---

### 🔴 Risky choices

- overcommitting to the bluff
- contradicting later coordination

---

## 🔑 Step 3: Manage the transition

When coordination improves:

- attribute it to the Poppy Grower dying
- adjust behavior accordingly
- maintain consistency

---

### Example plays

- “Early game felt messy — that could be Poppy Grower.”
- “If Poppy Grower died, that would explain why things feel sharper now.”

---

## 🔄 Mid-game as evil

### Stay flexible

If the bluff weakens:

- reduce reliance on it
- shift to normal explanations
- keep it as background support

---

### Use your claim strategically

- justify early mistakes
- delay suspicion
- explain changing game dynamics

---

## ⚠️ Common Mistakes (Evil)

### ❌ Overusing the bluff

It should support your play, not define it.

---

### ❌ Ignoring timing

Poppy Grower is a **timing-based explanation**.

---

### ❌ Misaligning behavior

Your play must match the claimed coordination state.

---

# 🧠 Advanced Notes

## 🔀 Information asymmetry

Poppy Grower affects:

- evil team knowledge
- coordination ability

but not:

- good information directly

---

## 🧩 Game flow impact

Poppy Grower creates two phases:

1. **Pre-death:** evil is blind and disorganized
2. **Post-death:** evil becomes informed and coordinated

---

## 🧠 Strategic impact

Poppy Grower shifts the game from:

- **immediate coordination**

to:

- **delayed coordination**

---

## 🧠 Key Insight

> **Poppy Grower does not weaken evil — it delays when they become dangerous.**
