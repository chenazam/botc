# Trouble Brewing

**Trouble Brewing** is the core introductory Blood on the Clocktower script.

It is often the first script players learn, but it remains strategically rich due to its clean structure and meaningful interactions.

---

## 🧠 Core Themes

### 🔍 Reliable Information (with exceptions)

Information is generally strong and actionable, but can be disrupted by:

- poisoning
- drunkenness
- evil bluffing

---

### ⚖️ Structured Logic

The game encourages:

- building clear worlds
- tracking claims
- validating information through consistency

---

### 🎭 Trust & Social Play

Even with strong mechanics, the game is often decided by:

- who is believed
- when information is shared
- how execution decisions are made

---

## 🧭 Script Identity

Trouble Brewing is built around:

- early information gathering
- outsider count and setup logic
- mechanical trust chains
- manageable but impactful misinformation

It rewards:

- structured world-building
- careful claim tracking
- testing assumptions
- understanding how misinformation distorts otherwise clean worlds

## 🎯 Strategic Themes

### Information is strong, but not absolute

Trouble Brewing often creates games where the town feels close to solving early. Misinformation can turn that confidence against the group.

---

### Setup matters

Outsider count, role claims, and execution outcomes reveal a great deal about the game’s structure.

---

### Endgames are sharp

Final three decisions are critical, with roles like Mayor, Saint, and hidden poisoning dramatically shifting optimal play.
