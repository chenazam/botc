# Demons (Trouble Brewing)

Trouble Brewing Demons are the primary evil threat and must balance:

- survival
- kill decisions
- bluff quality
- information control
- endgame planning

---

## 🧠 Design Philosophy

Trouble Brewing Demons are designed to:

- operate within a mostly **structured, information-driven game**
- survive long enough to reach final three
- support believable but incorrect worlds
- leverage both mechanical and social play

---

## 🔍 Core Characteristics

### 🛡️ Survival Focus

The Demon must:

- avoid execution
- maintain a believable identity
- manage suspicion over time

---

### 🔪 Kill Impact

Kills:

- remove threats
- shape the information landscape
- influence how the town interprets the game

---

### 🎭 Bluff Dependency

Unlike other roles:

- the Demon must always maintain a strong bluff
- identity consistency is critical

---

## 🧩 Role Breakdown

- [Imp](imp.md)

The Imp is simple in wording but deep in play, requiring:

- strong kill decisions
- careful social positioning
- coordination with Minions
- long-term planning

---

## 🎯 Playing the Demon

- Maintain a consistent and believable role identity
- Track how the town is building its world
- Support narratives that benefit your team
- Plan for the final three from early in the game

---

## 🎯 Kill Strategy

- Remove players who threaten your world
- Avoid kills that expose contradictions
- Consider leaving misinformation alive
- Use kills to reinforce a believable narrative
