# Outsiders (Trouble Brewing)

Trouble Brewing Outsiders are roles that complicate the game for good, primarily through **setup distortion and execution risk**.

They are central to:

- outsider count logic
- setup solving
- execution decisions
- hidden misinformation

---

## 🧠 Design Philosophy

Trouble Brewing Outsiders are designed to:

- affect **setup expectations**
- introduce **risk into executions**
- create **uncertainty around information**
- force players to consider alternative worlds

---

## 🔍 Core Characteristics

### ⚖️ Setup Impact

Outsiders influence:

- outsider count
- Baron presence
- expected role distribution

---

### ⚠️ Execution Risk

Some Outsiders:

- punish execution directly
- create uncertainty around “safe” kills

---

### 🎭 Hidden Disruption

Outsiders may:

- distort information
- create misleading signals
- interfere with otherwise clean logic

---

---

## 🎯 Playing Outsiders

- Be aware of how you affect setup logic
- Help the town interpret your presence correctly
- Avoid unnecessary execution risk
- Use your role to explain inconsistencies where appropriate

---

## 🎭 Bluffing Outsiders

Outsider bluffs are powerful because they:

- reduce expectations of useful information
- distort setup assumptions
- shift focus away from mechanical solving

However, they also:

- attract execution
- create count inconsistencies
- become dangerous in endgame
