# Empath (Trouble Brewing)

**Role Type:** Townsfolk  
**Ability:** Each night, you learn how many of your 2 alive neighbors are evil.

---

## 🧠 Overview

The Empath is a **recurring information role** that provides **ongoing alignment information about neighbors**.

You provide:

- a number (0, 1, or 2) each night based on your alive neighbors

Your power lies in:

- tracking changes over time
- confirming or challenging adjacent players
- detecting kills, poisons, and shifts in the game state

---

## 🗂️ Tags

- recurring-info

---

# 🟢 Playing Empath (Good)

## 🎯 Your Role

You are not just reporting numbers — you are:

> **Tracking alignment changes over time and anchoring local trust**

---

## 🟢 Day 1 Priorities

### 1. Share your information (usually early)

Default strong play:

> “I’m Empath, I got a 0.”  
> or  
> “I’m Empath, I got a 1.”

Why:

- early info helps establish neighbor trust or suspicion
- your information gains value over time
- early sharing allows others to interact with your data

---

### 2. Speak to your neighbors

Ask:

- “What are you claiming?”
- “What info do you have?”
- “Are you comfortable sharing publicly?”

You are evaluating:

- consistency with your number
- confidence and behavior
- how their claims fit into the wider game

---

### 3. Track seating carefully

You must always know:

- who your current alive neighbors are
- how deaths affect your information
- when your data should logically change

---

## 🧠 Mid-game Strategy

### Track changes across nights

Your strength comes from:

> **how your numbers evolve, not just what they are**

Example:

- 1 → 0 after a death → suggests an evil neighbor died

---

### Confirm or eliminate players

- consistent 0 → both neighbors likely good
- consistent 2 → both neighbors likely evil
- fluctuating results → requires deeper analysis

---

### Combine with other information

Empath works best with:

- Undertaker confirmations
- Chef constraints
- Investigator pairs
- execution outcomes

---

## ⚠️ Common Mistakes (Good)

### ❌ Ignoring changes over time

- a single number is weak
- patterns are strong

---

### ❌ Forgetting neighbor changes

- deaths shift your information
- always update your mental model

---

### ❌ Over-trusting your results

- poisoning can distort multiple nights of data

---

# 😈 Bluffing Empath (Evil)

## 🎯 Your Role

As evil, your goal is to:

> **Provide believable, evolving information that supports a false world**

---

## 🟢 Why Empath is a strong bluff

- flexible numbers (0, 1, 2)
- evolves over time
- difficult to prove wrong immediately
- interacts naturally with deaths and executions

---

## 🧩 Core Bluff

> “I’m Empath, I got a X.”

---

## 🔑 Step 1: Choose your starting number

### 🟢 0 (safe)

- low suspicion
- supports trust in neighbors

---

### 🟢 1 (balanced)

- creates mild suspicion
- very believable

---

### 🔴 2 (aggressive)

- high pressure
- difficult to sustain

---

## 🔑 Step 2: Plan your progression

Your numbers should:

- change logically with deaths
- align with your narrative
- avoid obvious contradictions

---

### Example progression

- Night 1: 1
- After neighbor dies: 0  
  → suggests evil neighbor was executed

---

## 🔑 Step 3: Use your info to shape the game

You can:

- protect evil neighbors (claim 0)
- frame good neighbors (claim 1 or 2)
- reinforce existing suspicion

---

### Example plays

- “My 1 has been consistent — one of my neighbors is still evil.”
- “After that execution, I went from 1 to 0 — that lines up.”

---

## 🔄 Mid-game as evil

### Stay flexible

If inconsistencies appear:

- suggest poisoning
- adjust your numbers carefully
- avoid rigid commitments

---

### Use your position strategically

- support your demon’s safety
- influence executions near you
- align your numbers with the dominant world

---

## ⚠️ Common Mistakes (Evil)

### ❌ Inconsistent progression

- your numbers must follow logical changes

---

### ❌ Ignoring deaths

- failing to adjust neighbors breaks your bluff

---

### ❌ Overcommitting to strong claims

- aggressive numbers (like repeated 2s) are hard to maintain

---

# 🧠 Advanced Notes

## 🔀 Poisoning / Drunkenness

Empath is highly sensitive to poisoning:

- multiple nights of incorrect data are possible
- false patterns can appear very convincing

---

## 🧩 Neighbor dynamics

Your role depends entirely on:

- who is alive
- where you are seated
- how deaths change your position

---

## 🧠 Social impact

Empath often:

- becomes a trusted or distrusted anchor
- influences nearby executions
- shapes local trust chains

---

## 🧠 Key Insight

> **Empath is not about one number — it is about the pattern across time.**
