# Townsfolk (Trouble Brewing)

Trouble Brewing Townsfolk are the foundation of the town’s ability to solve the game.

They provide:

- early information
- recurring information
- confirmation
- protection
- endgame leverage

---

## 🧠 Design Philosophy

Trouble Brewing Townsfolk are designed to:

- provide **clear, actionable information**
- support **structured world-building**
- create **trust chains and confirmations**
- reward **clean logical reasoning**

---

## 🔍 Core Characteristics

### 🔍 Clarity

Most Townsfolk:

- provide direct, understandable information
- are easy to interpret
- strongly influence early worlds

---

### 🔁 Accumulation

Information often:

- builds over time
- becomes more reliable when combined
- gains strength through consistency

---

### ⚠️ Vulnerability

Information can be:

- poisoned
- drunk
- misinterpreted

---

## 🧩 Role Breakdown

### Early information roles

- [Washerwoman](washerwoman.md)
- [Librarian](librarian.md)
- [Investigator](investigator.md)
- [Chef](chef.md)

These roles shape the early world and heavily influence Day 1–2.

---

### Recurring information roles

- [Empath](empath.md)
- [Fortune Teller](fortune_teller.md)
- [Undertaker](undertaker.md)

These roles grow stronger over time and often anchor mid-game worlds.

---

### Utility / protection roles

- [Monk](monk.md)
- [Ravenkeeper](ravenkeeper.md)
- [Virgin](virgin.md)
- [Slayer](slayer.md)
- [Soldier](soldier.md)

These roles shape execution flow, survivability, and confirmation.

---

### Social / endgame roles

- [Mayor](mayor.md)

Mayor introduces powerful endgame dynamics and alternative win conditions.

---

## 🎯 Playing Townsfolk

- Share information early and clearly
- Build structured worlds based on claims
- Track consistency across multiple players
- Be aware that even “clean” information can be wrong
