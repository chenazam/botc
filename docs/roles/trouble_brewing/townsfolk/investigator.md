# Investigator (Trouble Brewing)

**Role Type:** Townsfolk  
**Ability:** You start knowing that 1 of 2 players is a particular Minion.

---

## 🧠 Overview

The Investigator is an **early-game information role** that identifies **possible evil players**.

You provide:

- a pair of players, one of whom is a specific Minion

Your power lies in:

- introducing early suspicion
- identifying potential evil team members
- shaping the initial execution targets

---

## 🗂️ Tags

- early-info

---

# 🟢 Playing Investigator (Good)

## 🎯 Your Role

You are not just identifying evil — you are:

> **Creating the first concrete suspicion in the game**

---

## 🟢 Day 1 Priorities

### 1. Share your information (usually early)

Default strong play:

> “I’m Investigator, I saw either A or B as [Minion].”

Why:

- early suspicion is highly valuable
- it creates immediate pressure
- it forces reactions from both players

---

### 2. Speak to both players privately

Ask:

- “What are you claiming?”
- “What info do you have?”
- “Are you willing to claim publicly?”

You are evaluating:

- confidence
- willingness to engage
- consistency with other claims

---

### 3. Watch reactions carefully

Unlike Townsfolk pairs, one of these players is evil.

Look for:

- defensiveness or overreaction
- attempts to redirect suspicion
- inconsistent claims
- reluctance to commit

---

## 🧠 Mid-game Strategy

### Narrow your pair

Move from:

> “A or B”

to:

> **“I believe it is A, because…”**

---

### Apply pressure strategically

You should:

- push discussion around your pair
- encourage claims and counterclaims
- revisit your pair as new info emerges

---

### Coordinate with other information

Use:

- Empath
- Undertaker
- Washerwoman / Librarian

to confirm or challenge your suspicion

---

## ⚠️ Common Mistakes (Good)

### ❌ Treating your info as solved

- your info is not confirmation
- you still need to evaluate both players

---

### ❌ Ignoring alternative explanations

- poisoning or drunkenness may affect your info

---

### ❌ Over-tunneling your pair

- your info is important, but not the entire game

---

# 😈 Bluffing Investigator (Evil)

## 🎯 Your Role

As evil, your goal is to:

> **Create a believable early accusation that drives executions away from the real evil team**

---

## 🟢 Why Investigator is a strong bluff

- creates immediate pressure on others
- explains confident early accusations
- forces good players into defensive positions
- can shape the entire early game

---

## 🧩 Core Bluff

> “I’m Investigator, I saw either A or B as [Minion].”

---

## 🔑 Step 1: Choose your targets carefully

### 🟢 One good + one good (strong default)

- guarantees at least one wrong accusation
- creates internal conflict among good players

---

### 🟢 One good + one evil (advanced)

- allows protection of your teammate
- lets you push suspicion selectively

---

### 🚫 Avoid

- picking players already strongly trusted
- picking players with conflicting confirmed roles

---

## 🔑 Step 2: Choose the Minion

### 🟢 Safe choices

- Poisoner
- Scarlet Woman

---

### 🔴 Risky choices

- Spy (harder to fake interactions)
- Baron (interacts with setup logic)

---

## 🔑 Step 3: Drive the narrative

You are expected to push suspicion.

---

### Example plays

- “One of these two is definitely evil.”
- “If we execute one of them, we get strong information.”

---

## 🔄 Mid-game as evil

### Stay flexible

If your pair weakens:

- suggest poisoning or drunkenness
- reduce certainty
- shift focus to inconsistencies

---

### Use your pair strategically

- push executions on good players
- protect your teammate if included
- create ongoing doubt between players

---

## ⚠️ Common Mistakes (Evil)

### ❌ Picking an impossible pair

- contradictions will expose you quickly

---

### ❌ Overcommitting too early

- leave room to adapt if needed

---

### ❌ Ignoring the fallout

- your accusation shapes the game — manage it carefully

---

# 🧠 Advanced Notes

## 🔀 Poisoning / Drunkenness

If your info appears wrong:

- you may be poisoned
- or drunk

This can:

- falsely accuse good players
- create misleading but coherent worlds

---

## 🧩 Interaction with other roles

Investigator pairs often intersect with:

- Washerwoman / Librarian pairs
- Empath readings
- Undertaker confirmations

These overlaps are key to solving (or misleading)

---

## 🧠 Social impact

The Investigator often:

- drives the first execution
- sets the tone for suspicion
- creates early alignment narratives

---

## 🧠 Key Insight

> **Investigator does not find evil — it creates the first battle line between players.**
