# Minions (Trouble Brewing)

Trouble Brewing Minions support the evil team through **misinformation, setup distortion, and Demon protection**.

They shape:

- how the town interprets information
- which worlds feel plausible
- how resilient the evil team is

---

## 🧠 Design Philosophy

Trouble Brewing Minions are designed to:

- distort otherwise reliable information
- influence setup expectations
- protect the Demon from early collapse
- support structured but incorrect worlds

---

## 🔍 Core Characteristics

### 🧪 Information Disruption

Minions may:

- poison information
- introduce false signals
- create believable but incorrect worlds

---

### ⚖️ Setup Influence

Some Minions:

- alter outsider count
- change expected role distribution
- affect how the game is read mechanically

---

### 🛡️ Demon Support

Minions often:

- protect the Demon
- reduce risk of early loss
- stabilize the evil team

---

## 🧩 Role Breakdown

- [Poisoner](poisoner.md)
- [Spy](spy.md)
- [Scarlet Woman](scarlet_woman.md)
- [Baron](baron.md)

Each Minion plays a distinct role:

- **Poisoner** → ongoing misinformation
- **Spy** → hidden knowledge and social positioning
- **Scarlet Woman** → Demon protection and recovery
- **Baron** → setup manipulation and quieter influence

---

## 🎯 Playing Minions

- Support a consistent world for the town to believe
- Align your actions with your Demon’s needs
- Avoid unnecessary contradictions
- Adapt your role and behavior to the game state

---

## 🎭 Bluffing Minions

Minions require:

- flexible early claims
- believable role identities
- awareness of how the town is reasoning

A strong bluff should:

- not only be safe
- but actively support the evil team’s narrative
