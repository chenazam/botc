# Spy (Trouble Brewing)

**Role Type:** Minion  
**Ability:** Each night, you see the Grimoire. You might register as good and as a Townsfolk or Outsider, even if dead.

---

## 🧠 Overview

The Spy is an **information-dominant Minion** with full visibility of the game state.

You provide:

- perfect information about all players, roles, and game state

Your power lies in:

- complete knowledge of the game
- the ability to manipulate information with precision
- distorting trust by potentially appearing good

---

## 🗂️ Tags

- information
- high-impact
- high-complexity

---

# 😈 Playing Spy (Evil)

## 🎯 Your Role

You are not just informed — you are:

> **The strategic brain of the evil team**

---

## 🟢 Early-game Priorities

### 1. Process the Grimoire quickly

You should identify:

- all roles in play
- key information roles
- Outsider count and setup
- potential threats to your Demon

---

### 2. Choose a strong bluff

Your bluff should:

- fit the actual setup
- avoid conflicts
- support your team

---

### 3. Decide your level of activity

You can play:

- active (driving discussion)
- passive (blending in)

Choose based on:

- your group
- your confidence
- your role distribution

---

## 🧠 Mid-game Strategy

### Use your information precisely

You should:

- support correct-looking worlds
- avoid obvious contradictions
- guide the town subtly

---

### Protect your team

You can:

- redirect suspicion
- reinforce good-looking claims
- prevent dangerous executions

---

### Avoid overplaying

You know everything — but:

> **you must not appear to know everything**

---

## ⚠️ Common Mistakes (Evil)

### ❌ Using too much information

- makes you appear unnatural

---

### ❌ Playing perfectly

- perfect play can look suspicious

---

### ❌ Overcontrolling the game

- draws attention

---

# 🎭 Bluffing as Spy (Very Rare)

## 🎯 Your Role

In rare cases, your goal is to:

> **Leverage the idea of Spy to influence information sharing**

---

## 🟢 When this works

- when paranoia is already high
- when players are withholding information
- when confusion benefits your team

---

## 🧩 Core Bluff

> “If there’s a Spy, they already know everything.”

---

## ⚠️ Risks

- rarely necessary
- may confuse your own team
- can backfire if overused

---

# 🧠 Advanced Notes

## 🔀 Registration ability

Spy may:

- register as good
- register as Townsfolk or Outsider

This can:

- mislead information roles
- create false confirmations

---

## 🧩 Information discipline

Strong Spy play requires:

- selective sharing
- controlled influence
- restraint

---

## 🧠 Social impact

Spy often:

- changes how players share information
- increases paranoia
- disrupts normal trust patterns

---

## 🧠 Key Insight

> **Spy does not win by knowing everything — it wins by using only what it needs.**
