# Baron (Trouble Brewing)

**Role Type:** Minion  
**Ability:** There are extra Outsiders in play. [+2 Outsiders]

---

## 🧠 Overview

The Baron is a **setup-altering Minion** that increases the number of Outsiders in the game.

You provide:

- additional Outsiders that weaken the town
- structural misinformation about the setup

Your power lies in:

- distorting Outsider count logic
- reducing the number of Townsfolk
- creating uncertainty about the game’s structure

---

## 🗂️ Tags

- setup
- passive
- misinformation

---

# 😈 Playing Baron (Evil)

## 🎯 Your Role

You are not just adding Outsiders — you are:

> **Distorting the foundation of how the town understands the game**

---

## 🟢 Early-game Priorities

### 1. Understand the setup impact

You should know:

- how many Outsiders are expected
- how your presence changes that
- how the town might interpret it

---

### 2. Choose a safe bluff

You should:

- avoid roles that create pressure
- prefer low-risk or flexible roles

---

### 3. Avoid unnecessary attention

Default strong play:

- stay relatively low-profile

Why:

- your ability is already active
- you do not need to take risks

---

## 🧠 Mid-game Strategy

### Support false setup interpretations

You can:

- reinforce incorrect Outsider counts
- question correct assumptions
- introduce doubt

---

### Manage Outsider claims

You should:

- track who claims Outsider
- support believable distributions
- avoid contradictions

---

### Contribute without overexposing

You should:

- participate in solving
- avoid strong commitments
- maintain flexibility
