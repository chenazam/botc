# Scarlet Woman (Trouble Brewing)

**Role Type:** Minion  
**Ability:** If there are 5 or more players alive and the Demon dies, you become the Demon.

---

## 🧠 Overview

The Scarlet Woman is a **fail-safe Minion** that can become the Demon if the original Demon dies early.

You provide:

- continuity for the evil team
- protection against early Demon elimination

Your power lies in:

- allowing aggressive Demon play
- preserving the game after Demon death
- creating hidden transitions in control

---

## 🗂️ Tags

- support
- passive
- high-impact

---

# 😈 Playing Scarlet Woman (Evil)

## 🎯 Your Role

You are not just a backup — you are:

> **The safety net that allows the Demon to take risks**

---

## 🟢 Early-game Priorities

### 1. Stay alive

Default strong play:

- avoid attention
- avoid execution

Why:

- your ability only works while alive
- you must be present to take over

---

### 2. Blend in

You should:

- act like a normal Townsfolk
- avoid strong suspicion
- avoid standing out

---

### 3. Support the Demon indirectly

You should:

- reduce pressure on the Demon
- avoid drawing attention to the evil team
- help maintain stable worlds

---

## 🧠 Mid-game Strategy

### Be ready to transition

If the Demon dies:

- you become the Demon immediately
- you must adapt instantly

---

### Maintain flexibility

You should:

- avoid rigid claims
- leave room to change behavior
- be ready to take control

---

### Continue supporting your team

Even before transition:

- guide the game subtly
- protect your Demon
- maintain believable narratives

---

## ⚠️ Common Mistakes (Evil)

### ❌ Drawing attention early

- risks being executed before your ability matters

---

### ❌ Playing too passively

- you still need to contribute

---

### ❌ Not preparing for transition

- sudden role change requires readiness

---

# 🎭 Bluffing as Scarlet Woman (Rare)

## 🎯 Your Role

In rare cases, your goal is to:

> **Explain a sudden Demon transition or unusual survival**

---

## 🟢 When this matters

- after a Demon death
- when explaining unexpected game continuation

---

## 🧩 Core Bluff

> “There might be a Scarlet Woman in play.”

---

## ⚠️ Risks

- usually unnecessary
- may confuse the game state
- rarely needs explicit claiming

---

# 🧠 Advanced Notes

## 🔀 Demon transition

If the Demon dies while:

- 5 or more players are alive

→ Scarlet Woman becomes the Demon

This includes:

- execution
- Slayer shot
- self-kill (Imp star-pass)

---

## 🧩 Star-pass interaction

If the Imp kills themselves:

- Scarlet Woman becomes the new Demon

This allows:

- controlled Demon transfer
- repositioning of suspicion

---

## 🧠 Social impact

Scarlet Woman often:

- enables aggressive Demon strategies
- makes early Demon kills less decisive
- creates hidden shifts in game state

---

## 🧠 Key Insight

> **Scarlet Woman does not act — it ensures the evil team survives mistakes.**
