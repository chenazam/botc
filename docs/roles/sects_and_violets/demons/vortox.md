# Vortox (Sects and Violets)

**Role Type:** Demon  
**Ability:** Each night\*, choose a player: they die. Townsfolk abilities yield false information. Each day, if no one is executed, evil wins.

---

## 🧠 Overview

The Vortox is a Demon that enforces **global misinformation** and **mandatory execution**.

You provide:

- nightly deaths
- universally false Townsfolk information
- pressure to execute every day

Your power lies in:

- inverting all information
- forcing risky decisions
- punishing hesitation

---

## 🗂️ Tags

- misinformation
- global-effect
- high-pressure

---
