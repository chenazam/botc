# Fang Gu (Sects and Violets)

**Role Type:** Demon  
**Ability:** Each night\*, choose a player: they die. The 1st Outsider you kill becomes an evil Fang Gu & you die instead.

---

## 🧠 Overview

The Fang Gu is a Demon that can **jump into an Outsider**, creating a **new Demon mid-game**.

You provide:

- nightly deaths
- a potential Demon transfer
- Outsider conversion to evil

Your power lies in:

- escaping execution
- resetting suspicion
- creating sudden shifts in alignment

---

## 🗂️ Tags

- transformation
- high-impact
- swingy

---

# 😈 Playing Fang Gu (Evil)

## 🎯 Your Role

You are not just killing players — you are:

> **Planning when and how to transfer the Demon role**

---

## 🟢 Core Ability

- each night: kill a player
- first time you kill an Outsider:
  - they become the new Fang Gu (evil)
  - you die instead

---

## 🧠 Core Strategies

---

## 🔀 1. The Jump (Core Mechanic)

### Concept

Kill an Outsider to:

- pass the Demon role

---

### Result

- you die
- they become evil Demon

---

👉 This resets the game state

---

## 🧠 2. Timing the Jump

### Early jump

- high chaos
- harder for good to track

---

### Late jump

- dodge execution
- surprise endgame

---

👉 Timing is everything

---

## 🧠 3. Choose the Right Target

### Ideal Outsider

- trusted
- socially strong
- not suspected

---

👉 You create a powerful new Demon

---

## 🧠 4. Avoid Bad Targets

### Risky Outsiders

- heavily suspected
- socially weak
- likely to be executed

---

👉 You create a weak Demon

---

## 🧠 5. Bluff Around the Jump

### Concept

After jump:

- old Demon is dead
- new Demon must adapt quickly

---

👉 Strong communication helps

---

## ⚠️ Common Mistakes (Evil)

### ❌ Jumping too early without reason

- unnecessary chaos

---

### ❌ Choosing bad Outsiders

- weakens your position

---

### ❌ Failing to adapt after jump

- exposes new Demon

---

# 🟢 Playing Against Fang Gu (Good)

## 🎯 Your Role

You are not just identifying the Demon — you are:

> **Tracking whether the Demon has changed**

---

## 🧠 Core Strategies

---

## 🧠 1. Expect a jump

If Outsiders are present:

- Fang Gu is possible

---

## 🧠 2. Watch for sudden shifts

Clues:

- unexpected deaths
- sudden behavior changes
- new suspicious players

---

## 🧠 3. Re-evaluate after Demon death

If:

- Demon is executed or killed

But:

- game continues

---

👉 A jump likely occurred

---

## 🧠 4. Track Outsiders carefully

Outsiders:

- are potential Demon candidates

---

## ⚠️ Common Mistakes (Good)

### ❌ Assuming the game is solved

- Demon may have moved

---

### ❌ Ignoring Outsiders

- key targets

---

### ❌ Not adapting after jump

- outdated world

---

# 😈 Bluffing Fang Gu

## 🎯 Your Role

As evil, your goal is to:

> **Create confusion about Demon identity and timing**

---

## 🧩 Approach

You can:

- suggest a jump occurred
- deny a jump occurred
- manipulate Outsider perception

---

## 🧠 Advanced Notes

## 🔀 Jump condition

Only triggers:

- the first time you kill an Outsider

---

## 🧩 Alignment change

The Outsider:

- becomes evil
- becomes the new Demon

---

## 🧠 Social impact

Fang Gu often:

- creates dramatic shifts
- invalidates previous worlds
- rewards adaptability

---

## 🧠 Key Insight

> **Fang Gu wins by changing who the Demon is before the town realizes it.**
