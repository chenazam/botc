# Demons (Sects and Violets)

Sects and Violets Demons define the game through **uncertainty, misdirection, and control of information flow**.

Unlike simpler scripts:

- survival alone is not enough
- the Demon must actively shape the game state
- kills are as much about narrative as elimination

---

## 🧠 Design Philosophy

S&V Demons are designed to:

- interact with complex information systems
- manipulate how players interpret events
- create and maintain believable false worlds
- operate in environments where information is unstable

---

## 🔍 Core Characteristics

### 🎭 Narrative Control

Demons must:

- align kills with a believable story
- support specific worlds
- avoid creating contradictions

---

### 🔀 Indirect Influence

Demons influence:

- which players remain alive
- which information persists
- how the game evolves

---

### 🧩 Complexity

Compared to Trouble Brewing:

- decisions are less obvious
- optimal play is less clear
- adaptability is critical

## 🎯 Playing the Demon

- Think in terms of **worlds, not players**
- Adapt to changing information constantly
- Support ambiguity rather than clarity
- Reinforce multiple plausible realities

---

## 🎯 Kill Strategy

- Use kills to support your narrative
- Preserve misinformation sources
- Remove players who challenge your world
- Avoid making the game “too clean”
