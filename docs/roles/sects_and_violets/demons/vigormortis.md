# Vigormortis (Sects and Violets)

**Role Type:** Demon  
**Ability:** Each night\*, choose a player: they die. Minions you kill keep their ability & poison 1 Townsfolk neighbor.

---

## 🧠 Overview

The Vigormortis is a Demon that turns **dead Minions into ongoing sources of misinformation**.

You provide:

- nightly deaths
- persistent Minion abilities after death
- additional poisoning through dead Minions

Your power lies in:

- hiding Minion influence after death
- creating unexpected misinformation
- breaking the assumption that “dead = inactive”
