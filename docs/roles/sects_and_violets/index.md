# Sects and Violets

**Sects and Violets** is a script defined by **uncertainty, ambiguity, and information instability**.

Where Trouble Brewing rewards clean logic, Sects and Violets challenges players to navigate incomplete, conflicting, and shifting information.

---

## 🧠 Core Themes

### 🎭 Ambiguous Information

Many roles:

- provide multiple possibilities
- give partially true information
- require interpretation

---

### 🌀 Madness

Madness introduces behavioral constraints:

- players may be forced to act or claim specific roles
- breaking madness can result in penalties
- social play becomes critical

---

### 🔀 Identity Instability

Players may:

- change roles
- change alignment
- appear as something they are not

> **“Who is this player?” is often uncertain**

---

### 🧩 Persistent Uncertainty

- multiple worlds remain viable longer
- contradictions are harder to resolve
- false worlds can appear internally consistent

---

## 🧭 Script Identity

Sects and Violets is built around:

- interpreting ambiguous information
- managing misinformation and madness
- adapting to changing identities
- complex world-building under uncertainty

It rewards:

- flexible reasoning
- pattern recognition over single data points
- revisiting assumptions
- adapting to evolving game states

## 🎯 Strategic Themes

### Information is unstable

Even correct reasoning can lead to incorrect conclusions due to ambiguity and distortion.

---

### Certainty is dangerous

Overconfidence and early commitment are frequently punished.

---

### Identity must be questioned

Players are not always what they seem, and roles may change mid-game.

---

### Worlds must stay flexible

Winning requires adapting to new information and revising previous assumptions.
