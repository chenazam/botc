# Minions (Sects and Violets)

Sects and Violets Minions specialize in **information manipulation, identity disruption, and social control**.

They are not just support roles:

- they actively reshape how the game is understood
- they destabilize trust
- they make multiple worlds feel equally plausible

---

## 🧠 Design Philosophy

S&V Minions are designed to:

- interfere with information flow
- create believable false worlds
- amplify uncertainty and ambiguity
- control behavior and perception

---

## 🔍 Core Characteristics

### 🧪 Information Control

Minions may:

- alter results
- introduce false information
- create contradictions

---

### 🎭 Social Manipulation

Minions often:

- influence player behavior
- constrain communication (madness)
- redirect suspicion

---

### 🔀 Indirect Power

Many Minions:

- do not act openly
- instead shape the game subtly through others

---

---

## 🎯 Playing Minions

- support ambiguous and flexible worlds
- avoid creating clean, easily disproven narratives
- target key players to disrupt information flow
- align your actions with long-term game shaping

---

## 🎭 Bluffing Minions

Minion bluffs are strongest when they:

- embrace ambiguity rather than clarity
- adapt to changing information
- reinforce uncertainty instead of resolving it

Avoid:

- rigid or overly precise claims
- easily testable information
- creating a single “correct” world
