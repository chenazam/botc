# Pit-Hag (Sects and Violets)

**Role Type:** Minion  
**Ability:** Each night, choose a player & a character they become (if not in play). If a Demon is made, deaths tonight are arbitrary.

---

## 🧠 Overview

The Pit-Hag is a **role-manipulation Minion** that can **transform players into different characters**.

You provide:

- direct control over the game’s role composition
- the ability to reshape identities
- powerful disruption and creation of new worlds

Your power lies in:

- changing who players are
- creating or removing key abilities
- rewriting the structure of the game

---

## 🗂️ Tags

- role-manipulation
- chaos
- high-impact

---

# 😈 Playing Pit-Hag (Evil)

## 🎯 Your Role

You are not just causing chaos — you are:

> **Rewriting the game into a version where evil wins**

---

## 🧠 Core Strategies

---

## 🔀 1. Role Disruption (Basic)

### Concept

Turn a strong Townsfolk into:

- a weaker role
- or an Outsider

---

### Example

- Dreamer → Butler
- Artist → Klutz

---

👉 You remove impact from key players

---

## 🧠 2. Ability Removal

### Concept

Eliminate powerful information roles

---

### Example

- Dreamer → something else
- Savant → something else

---

👉 You reduce the town’s ability to solve

---

## 🧠 3. Outsider Injection

### Concept

Turn players into Outsiders

---

### Example

- convert someone into Klutz
- convert someone into Mutant

---

👉 You:

- increase confusion
- introduce risk

---

## 🧠 4. Create New Demons (Advanced)

### Concept

Turn a player into a Demon

---

### Effects

- new Demon exists
- deaths become arbitrary that night

---

👉 Extremely powerful but risky

---

## 🧠 5. Save Your Demon

### Concept

Turn your Demon into:

- a non-Demon role

Then:

- create a new Demon elsewhere

---

👉 You:

- dodge execution
- reset suspicion

---

## 🧠 6. Endgame Manipulation

### Concept

Reshape roles late game

---

### Example

- turn a key player into Klutz
- remove strong Townsfolk
- create confusion before final decisions

---

## ⚠️ Common Mistakes (Evil)

### ❌ Random transformations

- wastes your power

---

### ❌ Overusing chaos

- becomes obvious

---

### ❌ Ignoring role limits

- you cannot create duplicates

---

### ❌ Bad Demon creation

- can backfire

# 🧠 Advanced Notes

## 🔀 Role creation rules

You may:

- only create roles not currently in play

This requires:

- awareness of possible role distribution

---

## 🧩 Demon creation

If you create a Demon:

- that player becomes the Demon
- deaths that night are arbitrary

---

- creates identity confusion

- forces re-evaluation of all assumptions
