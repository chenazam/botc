# Witch (Sects and Violets)

**Role Type:** Minion  
**Ability:** Each night, choose a player: if they nominate tomorrow, they die.

---

## 🧠 Overview

The Witch is a **threat-based control role** that punishes players for **nominating**.

You provide:

- lethal pressure on player actions
- control over nominations
- disruption of normal game flow

Your power lies in:

- shaping who feels safe to nominate
- controlling execution dynamics
- creating hesitation and fear

---

## 🗂️ Tags

- control
- threat
- social-manipulation

---

# 😈 Playing Witch (Evil)

## 🎯 Your Role

You are not just killing players — you are:

> **Controlling who feels safe to take action**

---

## 🟢 Core Ability

Each night:

- choose a player

If they nominate the next day:

- they die

---

## 🧠 Core Strategies

---

## ⚡ 1. Threat Control (Basic)

### Concept

Target players likely to:

- nominate
- drive the game
- take initiative

---

### Result

They must:

- hesitate
- risk death
- change behavior

---

## 🧠 2. Silence Key Players

### Concept

Target strong or vocal players

---

### Example

- experienced player
- player leading discussion

---

👉 You:

- reduce their influence
- slow down the game

---

## 🧠 3. Punish Suspicion

### Concept

Target players likely to:

- nominate your Demon
- push executions

---

👉 You:

- discourage attacks
- protect your team

---

## 🧠 4. Create Fear

### Concept

Even without kills:

- players feel unsafe

---

👉 You influence:

- who nominates
- when nominations happen

---

## 🧠 5. Execution Disruption

### Concept

Make nominations risky

---

Result:

- fewer nominations
- delayed decisions
- confused execution flow

---

## ⚠️ Common Mistakes (Evil)

### ❌ Random targeting

- reduces impact

---

### ❌ Ignoring behavior patterns

- misses key targets

---

### ❌ Overusing obvious patterns

- makes Witch easy to identify

---

# 🟢 Bluffing Witch (Good)

_(rare, but possible in chaos-heavy games)_

## 🎯 Your Role

As good, your goal is to:

> **Explain sudden deaths or discourage nominations**

---

## 🧩 Use case

- explain unexpected deaths
- create caution
- shape nomination behavior

---

# 🧠 Advanced Notes

## 🔀 Trigger condition

Witch triggers when:

- the chosen player nominates

---

## 🧩 Timing

The effect:

- applies to the next day
- persists only for that day

---

## 🧠 Social impact

Witch often:

- creates hesitation
- slows down the game
- changes nomination patterns

---

## 🧠 Key Insight

> **Witch is not about killing players — it is about controlling their choices.**
