# Evil Twin (Sects and Violets)

**Role Type:** Minion  
**Ability:** You & an opposing player know each other. If the good player is executed, evil wins. If the evil player is executed, good wins.

---

## 🧠 Overview

The Evil Twin creates a **binary endgame condition** between two players:

- one is good
- one is evil

You both:

- know each other
- must convince the town

Your power lies in:

- forcing a 1v1 scenario
- shaping social perception
- controlling the narrative

---

## 🗂️ Tags

- social
- high-pressure
- endgame

---

# 😈 Playing Evil Twin (Evil)

## 🎯 Your Role

You are not just defending yourself — you are:

> **Forcing the game into a 1v1 that you can win**

---

## 🟢 Core Situation

- you know your twin
- they know you
- the town must decide

---

## 🧠 Core Strategies

---

## ⚡ 1. Claim early (often correct)

### Why

- sets the narrative
- forces the good twin to react
- gives you control

---

### BUT

This is NOT always correct.

---

## 🧠 2. Control the framing

You should define:

- what kind of player your twin is
- why they are suspicious
- what their “story” is

---

👉 You are writing the narrative

---

## 🧠 3. Undermine credibility

You should:

- question their consistency
- highlight contradictions
- attack their logic

---

---

## 🧠 4. Build support

You must:

- convince others
- not just win the 1v1

---

👉 The game is:

> “you vs them… in front of everyone”

---

## 🧠 5. Adapt your timing

### Early claim

- strong control
- high confidence

---

### Delayed claim

- reactive
- can disrupt expectations

---

👉 Both are valid depending on context

---

## ⚠️ Common Mistakes (Evil)

### ❌ Relying on “first claim = good” meta

- good players may hesitate
- you can still lose

---

### ❌ Playing only the 1v1

- you must convince the table

---

### ❌ Inconsistency

- fatal in a binary situation

---

# 🟢 Playing Evil Twin (Good)

## 🎯 Your Role

You are not just defending yourself — you are:

> **Convincing the town that your version of reality is correct**

---

## 🟢 Core Situation

- you know your twin
- they know you
- you must win the vote

---

## 🧠 Core Strategies

---

## 🧠 1. Stay calm

You should:

- avoid panic
- maintain consistency
- be believable

---

## 🧠 2. Use real information

You can:

- use your actual role
- support your claims with logic
- provide consistency

---

## 🧠 3. Build a coherent world

You must:

- explain how the game works
- show why your twin is evil
- make your world make sense

---

## 🧠 4. Address meta directly

If needed, say:

- “first claim doesn’t mean anything”
- “look at consistency instead”

---

## ⚠️ Common Mistakes (Good)

### ❌ Panic

- reduces credibility

---

### ❌ Overexplaining

- becomes suspicious

---

### ❌ Ignoring the table

- you need votes

---

# 🧠 Advanced Notes

## 🔀 Binary win condition

- execute evil twin → good wins
- execute good twin → evil wins

---

## 🧩 Social dynamics

Evil Twin often:

- becomes the center of the game
- overrides other information
- creates strong emotional pressure

---

## 🧠 Key Insight

> **Evil Twin is not about being correct — it is about being believed.**
