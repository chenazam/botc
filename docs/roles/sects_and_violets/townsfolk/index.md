# Townsfolk (Sects and Violets)

Sects and Violets Townsfolk provide **powerful but often ambiguous information**.

Unlike Trouble Brewing:

- information is rarely clean
- results often require interpretation
- abilities often introduce **uncertainty instead of clarity**

---

## 🧠 Design Philosophy

S&V Townsfolk are designed to:

- provide **high-impact but imperfect information**
- require **cross-referencing with other roles**
- create **multiple plausible worlds**
- reward **flexible reasoning over certainty**

---

## 🔍 Core Characteristics

### 🎭 Ambiguity

Many Townsfolk:

- provide multiple possible outcomes
- require interpretation
- cannot be trusted at face value

---

### 🔁 Interaction

Information:

- overlaps with other roles
- gains value when combined
- often only makes sense in context

---

### ⚠️ Fragility

Information can be:

- misleading
- incomplete
- distorted by other effects

---

---

## 🎯 Playing Townsfolk

- Do not rely on a single source of truth
- Avoid early commitment to one world
- Look for patterns across multiple roles
- Continuously revisit and update your assumptions
