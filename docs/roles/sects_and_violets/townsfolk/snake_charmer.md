# Snake Charmer (Sects and Violets)

**Role Type:** Townsfolk  
**Ability:** Each night, choose an alive player: a chosen Demon swaps characters & alignments with you, and you are then poisoned.

---

## 🧠 Overview

The Snake Charmer is a **high-risk, high-impact role** that can **swap alignment and role with the Demon**.

You provide:

- a potential instant win condition for the good team
- a powerful but dangerous disruption to the game state

Your power lies in:

- targeting the Demon correctly
- completely reshaping the game if successful
- introducing uncertainty into alignment and role identity

---

## 🗂️ Tags

- high-risk
- high-impact
- chaos

---

# 🟢 Playing Snake Charmer (Good)

## 🎯 Your Role

You are not just gathering information — you are:

> **Attempting to directly convert the Demon into a good player**

---

## 🟢 Early-game Priorities

### 1. Decide your risk tolerance

You must choose:

- aggressive play (early targeting)
- cautious play (wait for information)

---

### 2. Stay hidden

Default strong play:

- do not reveal early

Why:

- you are a high-value target
- your ability works best as a surprise
- being killed early removes your impact

---

### 3. Observe before acting (optional)

You may:

- wait for information to develop
- identify suspicious players
- reduce risk before targeting

---

## 🧠 Mid-game Strategy

### Target carefully

Your choice should consider:

- who is likely to be the Demon
- which players are high-risk targets
- how confident you are

---

### Understand the consequences

If you hit the Demon:

- you become the Demon
- they become the Snake Charmer
- you are poisoned

This creates:

- a major shift in the game
- potential confusion

---

### Manage information after a swap

If a swap occurs:

- the game state changes drastically
- you must adapt immediately

---

## ⚠️ Common Mistakes (Good)

### ❌ Targeting randomly

- very high risk

---

### ❌ Revealing too early

- reduces your effectiveness

---

### ❌ Not considering consequences

- a swap can create chaos if mismanaged

---

# 😈 Bluffing Snake Charmer (Evil)

## 🎯 Your Role

As evil, your goal is to:

> **Introduce fear and uncertainty around potential role swaps**

---

## 🟢 Why Snake Charmer is a strong bluff

- high-impact role → respected
- difficult to verify
- explains strange behavior
- creates paranoia

---

## 🧩 Core Bluff

> “I’m Snake Charmer.”

or

> “I might have hit the Demon.”

---

## 🔑 Step 1: Use ambiguity

You can:

- claim uncertainty
- suggest a swap may have occurred
- destabilize the game

---

## 🔑 Step 2: Create doubt

You can:

- question identities
- challenge assumptions
- introduce alternative worlds

---

## 🔑 Step 3: Stay flexible

You should:

- avoid committing to specific claims
- adapt your story as needed
- maintain plausible uncertainty

---

## ⚠️ Common Mistakes (Evil)

### ❌ Overcommitting to a swap story

- can be disproven

---

### ❌ Creating contradictions

- reduces credibility

---

### ❌ Overusing chaos

- can backfire

---

# 🧠 Advanced Notes

## 🔀 Swap mechanics

If Snake Charmer selects the Demon:

- roles and alignments swap
- the former Demon becomes good
- the Snake Charmer becomes evil
- the new Demon is poisoned

---

## 🧩 Poisoning after swap

After a successful swap:

- the new Demon is poisoned
- their ability may not function correctly

This creates:

- temporary instability
- additional confusion

---

## 🧠 Social impact

Snake Charmer often:

- creates paranoia
- introduces uncertainty about alignment
- reshapes the game dramatically

---

## 🧠 Key Insight

> **Snake Charmer is not about gathering information — it is about taking a calculated risk to change everything.**
