# Oracle (Sects and Violets)

**Role Type:** Townsfolk  
**Ability:** Each night, you learn how many dead players are evil.

---

## 🧠 Overview

The Oracle is a **recurring, clean information role** that tracks **how many dead players are evil**.

You provide:

- a number representing evil players among the dead

Your power lies in:

- confirming executions
- validating suspicions
- shaping late-game decisions

---

## 🗂️ Tags

- recurring-info
- clean
- confirmation

---

# 🟢 Playing Oracle (Good)

## 🎯 Your Role

You are not identifying evil directly — you are:

> **Tracking whether executed (or dead) players were actually evil**

---

## 🟢 Early-game Priorities

### 1. Share your numbers

Default strong play:

- share daily

Example:

> “Oracle: 1”

---

### 2. Track deaths carefully

You must:

- remember who died
- track execution order
- note night deaths

---

### 3. Stay grounded

Your info is:

- simple
- reliable
- powerful over time

---

## 🧠 Mid-game Strategy

### Validate executions

If:

- Oracle increases after an execution  
  → likely executed evil

If:

- Oracle stays the same  
  → likely executed good

---

### Track progression

Your numbers:

- form a timeline
- help identify when evil dies

---

### Combine with other info

Oracle works well with:

- Juggler
- Dreamer
- Artist
- general suspicions

---

## ⚠️ Common Mistakes (Good)

### ❌ Ignoring timing

- when the number changes matters

---

### ❌ Forgetting previous results

- pattern is key

---

### ❌ Overinterpreting early

- early game is noisy

---

# 😈 Bluffing Oracle (Evil)

## 🎯 Your Role

As evil, your goal is to:

> **Provide believable confirmation-style information**

---

## 🟢 Why Oracle is a strong bluff

- simple numbers
- easy to track
- fits many game states
- believable

---

## 🧩 Core Bluff

> “Oracle: X”

---

## 🔑 Step 1: Match deaths

You must:

- track dead players
- ensure numbers make sense

---

## 🔑 Step 2: Support your team

You can:

- confirm evil as “good”
- mislead about executions
- shape narratives

---

## 🔑 Step 3: Stay consistent

Your numbers must:

- follow a believable progression
- align with events

---

## ⚠️ Common Mistakes (Evil)

### ❌ Incorrect counts

- easily exposed

---

### ❌ Ignoring timeline

- breaks credibility

---

### ❌ Overcomplicating

- unnecessary

---

# 🧠 Advanced Notes

## 🔀 What counts

Oracle counts:

- all dead players
- regardless of how they died

---

## 🧩 Interpretation

Oracle does not tell you:

- which dead players are evil
- when exactly evil died

---

## 🧠 Social impact

Oracle often:

- validates executions
- reinforces or challenges suspicion
- stabilizes chaotic games

---

## 🧠 Key Insight

> **Oracle does not tell you who is evil — it tells you whether you’ve been right so far.**
