# Outsiders (Sects and Violets)

Sects and Violets Outsiders introduce **uncertainty, instability, and social pressure**.

They do not just weaken the town — they actively reshape how information must be interpreted.

---

## 🧠 Design Philosophy

S&V Outsiders are designed to:

- disrupt clean logic
- introduce alternative explanations
- distort information interpretation
- create persistent uncertainty

---

## 🔍 Core Characteristics

### 🌀 Information Distortion

Outsiders may:

- produce misleading signals
- interfere with other roles
- create false but believable patterns

---

### 🎭 Social Pressure

Some Outsiders:

- constrain behavior
- influence how players communicate
- create risk in sharing information

---

### 🔀 Interpretive Uncertainty

Outsiders often:

- make it unclear what information is reliable
- create multiple valid explanations
- prevent clean world resolution

---

## 🧩 Role Breakdown

- [Mutant](mutant.md)
- [Sweetheart](sweetheart.md)
- [Barber](barber.md)

- introduces a different type of instability
- affects either information, identity, or decision-making

---

## 🎯 Playing Outsiders

- Accept that your role introduces uncertainty
- Help the group interpret inconsistencies
- Avoid amplifying confusion unnecessarily
- Use your presence to explain contradictions

---

## 🎭 Bluffing Outsiders

Outsider bluffs are strong because they:

- justify incorrect or conflicting information
- create alternative explanations
- reduce pressure to provide clear information

However, they also:

- invite suspicion
- can collapse under scrutiny
- become risky in endgame situations
