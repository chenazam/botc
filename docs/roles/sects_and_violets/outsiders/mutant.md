# Mutant (Sects and Violets)

**Role Type:** Outsider  
**Ability:** If you are “mad” about being an Outsider, you might be executed.

---

## 🧠 Overview

The Mutant is a **madness-based Outsider** that is punished for **openly acknowledging its role type**.

You provide:

- uncertainty about Outsider presence
- social pressure and behavioral constraints

Your power lies in:

- managing how you present yourself
- avoiding execution while restricted
- creating ambiguity around Outsider claims

---

## 🗂️ Tags

- madness
- social
- high-risk

---

# 🟢 Playing Mutant (Good)

## 🎯 Your Role

You are not just hiding your role — you are:

> **Convincingly acting like you are NOT an Outsider**

---

## 🟢 Core Rule

You must NOT:

- openly claim to be an Outsider
- strongly imply you are an Outsider

If you do:

- you may be executed immediately

---

## 🟢 Early-game Priorities

### 1. Choose a cover identity

You should:

- behave like a Townsfolk
- make believable claims
- avoid suspicion

---

### 2. Avoid obvious Outsider behavior

You must:

- not hint too strongly at being harmful
- not openly claim Outsider status

---

### 3. Stay consistent

Your story must:

- make sense
- hold up under pressure
- align with your behavior

---

## 🧠 Mid-game Strategy

### Balance honesty and survival

You may:

- hint subtly
- avoid direct lies if possible

But must:

- never clearly break madness

---

### Manage suspicion

If suspected:

- defend yourself as a Townsfolk
- avoid collapsing into obvious Outsider behavior

---

### Work with the town indirectly

You can:

- contribute to discussion
- support good decisions
- avoid drawing execution

---

## ⚠️ Common Mistakes (Good)

### ❌ Claiming Outsider

- immediate risk of execution

---

### ❌ Overcorrecting

- acting too defensive or strange

---

### ❌ Breaking under pressure

- panic reveals your role

---

# 😈 Bluffing Mutant (Evil)

## 🎯 Your Role

As evil, your goal is to:

> **Use madness pressure to manipulate behavior and create confusion**

---

## 🟢 Why Mutant is a strong bluff

- madness creates uncertainty
- difficult to verify
- influences social dynamics
- explains strange behavior

---

## 🧩 Core Bluff

Indirect:

> “I’m not an Outsider.”

or

> behaving as if you cannot say something

---

## 🔑 Step 1: Simulate constraint

You should:

- act restricted
- avoid certain statements
- appear pressured

---

## 🔑 Step 2: Use social tension

You can:

- create awkward situations
- force others to interpret your behavior
- introduce doubt

---

## 🔑 Step 3: Stay believable

Your behavior must:

- feel natural
- not be exaggerated
- remain consistent

---

## ⚠️ Common Mistakes (Evil)

### ❌ Overacting madness

- becomes obvious

---

### ❌ Contradicting yourself

- reduces credibility

---

### ❌ Forcing the narrative

- feels unnatural

---

# 🧠 Advanced Notes

## 🔀 What is “madness”

Madness is:

- a behavioral expectation
- enforced by the Storyteller

It is not:

- a strict rule system
- but a social contract

---

## 🧩 Execution risk

The Storyteller may:

- execute you for breaking madness
- choose timing and severity

---

## 🧠 Social impact

Mutant often:

- creates tension
- introduces uncertainty
- influences how players communicate

---

## 🧠 Key Insight

> **Mutant is not about hiding information — it is about controlling how your behavior is interpreted.**
