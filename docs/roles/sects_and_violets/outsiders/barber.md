# Barber (Sects and Violets)

**Role Type:** Outsider  
**Ability:** If you died today or tonight, the Demon may choose 2 players (not another Demon) to swap characters.

---

## 🧠 Overview

The Barber is an Outsider that enables the Demon to **swap the roles of two players upon its death**.

You provide:

- a potential reshaping of the entire game state

Your impact lies in:

- introducing identity instability
- invalidating assumptions about who is who
- creating new worlds mid-game

---

- disruptive

# 🧠 Advanced Notes

## 🔀 Swap mechanics

If Barber dies:

- the Demon may choose two players
- their characters are swapped

Restrictions:

- cannot involve another Demon

---

## 🧩 Effects of swapping

After a swap:

- roles belong to different players
- previous information may still be correct
- but attached to the wrong person

---

## 🧠 Interpretation challenge

Barber creates:

- worlds where information is correct
- but assigned incorrectly

---

## 🧠 Social impact

Barber often:

- destabilizes trust
- creates multiple valid worlds
- forces players to rethink assumptions

---

## 🧠 Key Insight

> **Barber does not create false information — it moves true information to the wrong place.**
