# Po (Bad Moon Rising)

**Role Type:** Demon  
**Ability:** Each night\*, you may choose a player: they die. If your last choice was no-one, choose 3 players tonight.

---

## 🧠 Overview

The Po is a **tempo-based Demon** that trades **skipped kills for explosive multi-kills**, creating dramatic swings in the game state.

You provide:

- controlled kill pacing
- sudden burst damage (3 kills)
- psychological pressure through uncertainty

Your power lies in:

- choosing when to hold and when to strike
- manipulating expectations around death
- creating high-impact turning points

---

# 😈 Playing Po (Evil)

## 🎯 Your Role

You are not just killing players — you are:

> **Controlling the rhythm of the game, then breaking it**

---

## 🟢 Early Game Strategy

### 1. Decide whether to charge early

Default mindset:

> “Is it worth skipping a kill now to gain power later?”

You should consider:

- current game state
- number of players
- how valuable a burst would be

---

### 2. Use skipped nights strategically

Skipping a kill:

- creates a no-death night
- may be explained by other roles
- builds toward a powerful turn

---

### 3. Blend into the script

You want:

- no-death nights to feel natural
- your charge to go unnoticed
- the town to misinterpret the cause

---

## 🧠 Mid-game Strategy

### Time your burst

Move from:

> “I can kill three players.”

to:

> **“When does killing three players win or break the game?”**

---

### Choose targets carefully

A strong Po burst:

- removes key players
- destabilizes the town
- forces panic and mistakes

---

### Use psychology

You can:

- lull the town into safety
- create false confidence
- punish overcommitment

---

## ⚠️ Common Mistakes (Evil)

### ❌ Charging at the wrong time

A poorly timed burst may not have enough impact.

---

### ❌ Choosing weak targets

Your triple kill must matter.

---

### ❌ Making the pattern obvious

If the town expects the burst, it loses power.

---

# 🟢 Bluffing as Good (Po Cover)

## 🎯 Your Goal

As the Demon, your bluff should:

> **Explain both no-death nights and sudden multi-death nights**

---

## 🟢 Strong bluff choices

Po pairs well with:

- Exorcist (no deaths)
- protection roles (no deaths)
- Gossip / Gambler (extra deaths)

---

## 🧩 Bluff Strategy

You should:

- explain no-death nights through interference
- explain multi-death nights through chaos
- avoid revealing a clear pattern

---

### Example plays

- “No deaths could be Exorcist or protection.”
- “Multiple deaths could be Gossip or something else.”

---

## 🔄 Mid-game Bluffing

### Stay flexible

- adapt explanations
- avoid committing to one cause
- support multiple interpretations

---

### Use your position carefully

- guide the narrative subtly
- avoid drawing attention
- maintain ambiguity

---

## ⚠️ Common Mistakes (Bluffing)

### ❌ Ignoring the pattern

Your bluff must account for both phases of your ability.

---

### ❌ Overexplaining

Let the chaos cover your actions.

---

### ❌ Drawing attention

A visible pattern can expose you.

---

- alternating tempo

- high-impact turns
