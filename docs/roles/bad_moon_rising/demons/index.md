# Demons (Bad Moon Rising)

Bad Moon Rising Demons define the game through **tempo swings, unusual death patterns, and pressure on survival logic**.

Unlike simpler scripts:

- one night can radically change the game
- kill patterns are part of the Demon’s identity
- surviving is only part of the challenge

---

## 🧠 Design Philosophy

Bad Moon Rising Demons are designed to:

- shape the game through distinctive kill behavior
- create dramatic shifts in tempo
- pressure the town through night outcomes
- force players to interpret survival and death carefully

---

## 🔍 Core Characteristics

### 🔪 Kill Pattern Identity

Each Demon:

- changes how deaths happen
- creates a recognizable pressure pattern
- forces different kinds of worlds

---

### 🌙 Tempo Control

Demons influence:

- how fast the game moves
- whether nights feel safe or explosive
- how much room the town has to stabilize

---

### 🎭 Narrative Through Deaths

In Bad Moon Rising, deaths themselves are a language.
The Demon communicates through:

- who dies
- how many die
- when nobody dies

## 🎯 Playing the Demon

- Think in terms of what the night should “say”
- Use kill patterns to support your world
- Exploit volatility instead of avoiding it
- Plan not just for survival, but for pace

---

## 🎯 Kill Strategy

- Use deaths to communicate a believable story
- Consider whether speed or restraint benefits you more
- Remove players who can correctly explain the night
- Avoid patterns that make your identity too obvious too early
