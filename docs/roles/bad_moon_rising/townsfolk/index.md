# Townsfolk (Bad Moon Rising)

Bad Moon Rising Townsfolk are the core of the script’s ability to interpret **night deaths, protection, and survival patterns**.

They provide:

- recurring information
- death prevention
- kill control
- night-based confirmation
- tempo shaping

---

## 🧠 Design Philosophy

Bad Moon Rising Townsfolk are designed to:

- make night outcomes meaningful
- reward careful interpretation of survival and death
- create powerful but timing-sensitive effects
- interact heavily with the script’s volatility

---

## 🔍 Core Characteristics

### 🌙 Night Dependence

Many Townsfolk:

- act at night
- gain value from survival
- depend on repeated cycles to become strong

---

### 🛡️ Protective Influence

Protection and survival roles are especially important in this script:

- unexpected survival is common
- death patterns are often non-obvious
- tempo matters

---

### ⚠️ Interpretive Difficulty

Townsfolk information can be:

- powerful
- delayed
- highly contextual

Night outcomes often require explanation rather than simple observation.

## 🎯 Playing Townsfolk

- Track nights carefully, not just days
- Pay attention to survival as much as death
- Avoid overcommitting to obvious explanations
- Use timing and repetition to strengthen conclusions

---

## 🎭 Bluffing Townsfolk

Town bluffing in Bad Moon Rising often works well because:

- many roles are night-based
- many outcomes are hard to verify immediately
- unusual survival is already expected

However, strong Town bluffs must:

- fit death patterns
- remain consistent over time
- not collapse under night-by-night review
