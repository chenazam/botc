# Outsiders (Bad Moon Rising)

Bad Moon Rising Outsiders complicate the game through **death risk, survival distortion, and execution pressure**.

They do not just weaken the town — they often make the script’s volatile night structure harder to interpret.

---

## 🧠 Design Philosophy

Bad Moon Rising Outsiders are designed to:

- distort death interpretation
- create risk around execution and survival
- punish incorrect assumptions
- make volatile game states harder to solve cleanly

---

## 🔍 Core Characteristics

### ☠️ Death Pressure

Outsiders may:

- change how deaths should be interpreted
- punish incorrect executions
- turn survival into a liability

---

### 🔀 Hidden Consequences

Their effects are often:

- delayed
- indirect
- difficult to spot immediately

---

### ⚠️ Volatility Amplification

Bad Moon Rising is already unstable; Outsiders often make it even harder to read.

## 🎯 Playing Outsiders

- Be aware of how your role affects death interpretation
- Avoid unnecessary chaos when possible
- Help the group understand how your presence changes the game
- Think about timing, not just identity

---

## 🎭 Bluffing Outsiders

Outsider bluffs are strong in Bad Moon Rising because:

- the script already contains chaotic night outcomes
- players expect strange survival and death patterns
- uncertainty is normal

However, they can still:

- attract execution
- force risky claims
- become dangerous in endgame worlds
