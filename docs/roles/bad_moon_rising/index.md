# Bad Moon Rising

**Bad Moon Rising** is a Blood on the Clocktower script defined by **night deaths, hidden protection, and high-lethality uncertainty**.

Where Trouble Brewing emphasizes clean structure and Sects and Violets emphasizes ambiguity, Bad Moon Rising is built around survival, death patterns, and the difficulty of interpreting what happened overnight.

---

## 🧠 Core Themes

### 🌙 Night Action Intensity

Many roles in Bad Moon Rising act at night, and those actions often:

- prevent deaths
- cause extra deaths
- distort what night outcomes mean

---

### ☠️ Death Pattern Interpretation

The script frequently produces:

- multiple deaths
- no deaths
- unusual death timing

This means:

- the number of deaths matters
- but the obvious explanation is often wrong

---

### 🛡️ Protection and Survival

Protection is a major part of the script:

- players may survive unexpectedly
- strong roles may be preserved
- Demons must navigate hidden defenses

---

### 🔁 Volatile Game States

Bad Moon Rising swings hard:

- worlds can change quickly
- one night can completely reshape the game
- certainty is fragile

---

## 🧭 Script Identity

Bad Moon Rising is built around:

- interpreting night deaths
- hidden protection and survival
- highly influential recurring night actions
- balancing risk, tempo, and information

It rewards:

- careful night-by-night analysis
- resisting obvious conclusions
- adapting to volatile game states
- thinking about what _caused_ deaths, not just who died

## 🎯 Strategic Themes

### Night outcomes matter more than claims alone

Bad Moon Rising is often solved through:

- death counts
- survival anomalies
- action timing

---

### Protection creates false confidence

A player surviving does not always mean:

- they are evil
- they were ignored
- the Demon made a mistake

Protection and interference are common.

---

### Volatility punishes rigid worlds

A world that feels correct can collapse after a single night.

---

### Tempo matters

The pace of the game can shift rapidly based on:

- extra deaths
- prevented kills
- role timing
- Demon choice patterns
