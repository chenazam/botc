# Minions (Bad Moon Rising)

Bad Moon Rising Minions support the evil team through **night pressure, death manipulation, and game-state distortion**.

They shape:

- how safe players feel
- how deaths are interpreted
- how much control good has over the pace of the game

---

## 🧠 Design Philosophy

Bad Moon Rising Minions are designed to:

- intensify night pressure
- distort the meaning of deaths and survival
- support aggressive or deceptive Demon play
- exploit the script’s volatility

---

## 🔍 Core Characteristics

### 🌙 Night Influence

Minions often:

- affect what happens at night
- influence who feels safe
- create or exploit unusual outcomes

---

### 🎭 Pressure and Fear

Minions can:

- punish risky actions
- create hesitation
- change how players use their abilities

---

### 🔀 Hidden Leverage

Their influence is often strongest when:

- not fully visible
- mistaken for Demon activity
- interpreted as “normal chaos”

---

## 🧩 Role Breakdown

- [Godfather](godfather.md)
- [Devil's Advocate](devils_advocate.md)
- [Assassin](assassin.md)
- [Mastermind](mastermind.md)

Each Minion:

- pressures the town differently
- changes tempo, survival, or execution meaning
- supports a different style of Demon play

---

## 🎯 Playing Minions

- Think about tempo, not just disruption
- Support a coherent explanation for strange night outcomes
- Help your Demon survive volatility
- Use pressure to influence player decisions before they act

---

## 🎭 Bluffing Minions

Strong Minion bluffs in Bad Moon Rising should:

- fit the death pattern of the game
- account for unusual survival
- avoid creating contradictions with night outcomes
- support the Demon’s overall pace and narrative
