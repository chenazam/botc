# Godfather (Bad Moon Rising)

**Role Type:** Minion  
**Ability:** You start knowing which Outsiders are in play. If 1 died today, you kill tonight. [+1 Outsider]

---

## 🧠 Overview

The Godfather is an **information + conditional kill Minion** that gains power from **Outsider presence and deaths**.

You provide:

- knowledge of Outsiders in play
- an extra kill when Outsiders die by execution
- pressure on Outsider claims and executions

Your power lies in:

- leveraging Outsider information
- influencing execution decisions
- shaping night death patterns

---

# 😈 Playing Godfather (Evil)

## 🎯 Your Role

You are not just a Minion — you are:

> **A controller of tempo who turns Outsider deaths into lethal advantage**

---

## 🟢 Early Game Strategy

### 1. Use your Outsider information

Default mindset:

> “I know something the town doesn’t — I can shape the narrative.”

You should:

- track Outsider claims
- identify real vs fake claims
- detect inconsistencies

---

### 2. Influence execution choices

You want:

- Outsiders to be executed when beneficial
- confusion around Outsider identity
- the town to misread Outsider presence

---

### 3. Coordinate with your Demon

You should:

- align kill patterns
- avoid redundant targets
- maximize pressure

---

## 🧠 Mid-game Strategy

### Convert Outsider deaths into kills

Move from:

> “An Outsider died.”

to:

> **“This gives us an extra kill tonight — how do we use it best?”**

---

### Shape the narrative

You can:

- hide your extra kill within chaos
- create misleading death patterns
- exploit the town’s assumptions

---

### Use timing strategically

An extra kill can:

- accelerate the game
- break a stable world
- eliminate key players

---

## ⚠️ Common Mistakes (Evil)

### ❌ Wasting the extra kill

Poor targeting reduces your impact significantly.

---

### ❌ Ignoring Outsider claims

Your power depends on correctly reading them.

---

### ❌ Creating obvious patterns

Extra kills should blend into the game, not stand out.

---

# 🟢 Bluffing as Good (Godfather Cover)

## 🎯 Your Goal

As evil, your bluff should:

> **Explain death patterns while supporting your team’s narrative**

---

## 🟢 Strong bluff choices

Godfather often pairs well with:

- roles that explain extra deaths (Gossip, Gambler)
- roles that interact with night patterns
- roles that create ambiguity

---

## 🧩 Bluff Strategy

You should:

- explain extra deaths through other roles
- avoid drawing attention to Outsiders
- maintain consistency with the game state

---

### Example plays

- “That extra death could be Gossip.”
- “This night was messy — multiple things could explain it.”

---

## 🔄 Mid-game Bluffing

### Stay subtle

- avoid revealing too much control
- let chaos explain your actions
- support believable worlds

---

### Use Outsider knowledge carefully

- guide the town without exposing yourself
- subtly challenge or support claims
- avoid appearing too informed

---

## ⚠️ Common Mistakes (Bluffing)

### ❌ Overcontrolling the narrative

Too much influence can make you suspicious.

---

### ❌ Ignoring consistency

Your explanations must match observed events.

---

### ❌ Revealing hidden knowledge

If you appear to know too much, you risk exposure.

---
