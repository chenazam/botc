# Glossary

## Bluff

A false role claim used to appear good.

## Double claim

A situation where two players claim the same role.

## Drunk

A player who believes they are a Townsfolk but has no real ability.

## Poisoned

A player whose ability gives false information or fails to work correctly while poisoned.

## World

A hypothetical explanation of the game state.

## Trust chain

A sequence of players or claims that reinforce each other as likely good.

## Setup logic

Reasoning based on role counts, Outsider claims, and script structure.

## Final three

The last day phase with three players alive, often requiring special decision logic.
