# When to Trust Information

Information is the primary tool for solving a game of Blood on the Clocktower.

But not all information is equally reliable — and trusting it incorrectly is one of the most common ways to lose.

---

## 🧠 Core Principle

> **Information is only as reliable as the system that produced it.**

---

## 🎯 Why This Matters

Many players:

- trust information too quickly
- discard information too aggressively
- fail to adapt their trust to the script

Strong play requires:

- evaluating _how_ information was generated
- understanding _when_ it may be wrong
- adjusting trust dynamically

---

# 🟢 Trouble Brewing — Trust First, Then Verify

In Trouble Brewing, most information is:

- reliable
- consistent
- logically structured

---

## 🧩 How to approach it

You should:

- assume information is correct
- build worlds around it
- discard worlds when contradictions appear

---

## ⚠️ When to doubt

- poisoning (Poisoner)
- drunkenness (Drunk)
- rare inconsistencies

---

## 🎯 Summary

> **Trust information by default — but verify it through world consistency.**

---

# 🟣 Sects and Violets — Trust Carefully

In Sects and Violets, information is:

- often ambiguous
- sometimes misleading
- shaped by interactions

---

## 🧩 How to approach it

You should:

- treat information as _potentially true_
- avoid committing too early
- compare multiple interpretations

---

## ⚠️ When to doubt

- madness
- role interactions
- alignment shifts

---

## 🎯 Summary

> **Treat information as a possibility — not a conclusion.**

---

# 🔴 Bad Moon Rising — Trust Last

In Bad Moon Rising, information is:

- frequently distorted
- heavily context-dependent
- influenced by multiple overlapping effects

---

## 🧩 How to approach it

You should:

- interpret outcomes before trusting claims
- analyze cause and effect
- rely on patterns, not isolated data

---

## ⚠️ When to doubt

- almost always

---

## 🎯 Summary

> **Trust information only after understanding what caused it.**

---

# 🔄 Comparing the Three

| Script            | Default Trust Level | Approach               |
| ----------------- | ------------------- | ---------------------- |
| Trouble Brewing   | High                | Trust → verify         |
| Sects and Violets | Medium              | Evaluate → compare     |
| Bad Moon Rising   | Low                 | Interpret → then trust |

---

# ⚠️ Common Mistakes

## ❌ Overtrusting

Believing information without context.

---

## ❌ Undertrusting

Ignoring useful information entirely.

---

## ❌ Static thinking

Using the same trust model in every script.

---

# 🎯 Practical Heuristic

When evaluating information, ask:

1. How was this information generated?
2. What could distort it?
3. Does it fit with the rest of the game?
4. How much trust does this script allow?

---

# 🧠 Final Insight

> **Good players don’t just gather information — they understand when it deserves to be believed.**
