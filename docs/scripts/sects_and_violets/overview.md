# Sects and Violets

Sects and Violets is a script focused on **ambiguity, madness, and unstable information**.

It is a step up in complexity from Trouble Brewing and rewards:

- flexible thinking
- careful interpretation
- strong social awareness

---

## 🧠 What Makes This Script Unique

### 🎭 Madness

Some roles impose behavioral constraints:

- players may be forced to claim or act in specific ways
- breaking madness can have consequences
- social play becomes critical

---

### 🌀 Ambiguous Information

Many roles:

- provide multiple possible answers
- create uncertainty rather than clarity
- require cross-referencing

---

### 🔀 Identity and Alignment Complexity

Players may:

- appear differently to others
- change roles or alignment
- create misleading signals

---

## 🧭 Core Themes

### 1. Information is unstable

Even correct reasoning can lead to incorrect conclusions.

---

### 2. Certainty is dangerous

Overconfidence and early commitment are frequently punished.

---

### 3. Identity is not fixed

Players are not always what they seem, and roles may change mid-game.

---

### 4. Worlds must stay flexible

Multiple worlds often remain viable deep into the game.

---

## 🎯 How to Approach the Script

### As Good

- Build flexible, revisable worlds
- Avoid committing too early
- Look for patterns across multiple sources
- Re-evaluate assumptions constantly

---

### As Evil

- Reinforce ambiguity
- Support multiple plausible worlds
- Exploit contradictions
- Disrupt clean reasoning

---

## ⚠️ Common Good-Player Mistakes

- committing too early to one world
- trusting information at face value
- failing to revisit assumptions
- ignoring the possibility of identity changes

---

## 😈 Common Evil Goals

- create multiple believable worlds
- prevent clean resolution
- introduce controlled contradictions
- maintain uncertainty
- guide the game into a confused endgame
