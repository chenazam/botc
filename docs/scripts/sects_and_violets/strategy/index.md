# Strategy (Sects and Violets)

This section collects focused strategy guides for **Sects and Violets**.

Sects and Violets rewards players who can:

- manage conflicting and ambiguous information
- maintain multiple possible worlds at once
- navigate social pressure and behavioral constraints

Strategy in Sects and Violets most often revolves around:

- interpreting conflicting information without forcing conclusions
- managing madness and social pressure
- adapting to shifting identity and alignment
- keeping multiple worlds viable over time
