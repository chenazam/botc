# All Sources of Death (Bad Moon Rising)

In Bad Moon Rising, death is the most important — and most misleading — signal in the game.

---

## 🧠 Core Principle

> **A death tells you that something happened — not what happened.**

---

## 🎯 Why This Matters

Unlike other scripts:

- many roles can cause death
- deaths may be delayed
- deaths may be prevented
- deaths may be reversed

---

# 🔪 Demon Sources

- standard Demon kills
- Po (burst kills)
- Shabaloth (double kill)
- Pukka (delayed kill)

---

# ⚠️ Non-Demon Sources

- Gossip
- Gambler
- Godfather
- Tinker
- Moonchild

---

# 🛡️ Death Prevention

- Sailor
- Innkeeper
- Tea Lady
- Fool
- Exorcist

---

# 🔄 Death Alteration

- Pukka (delay)
- Shabaloth (resurrection)
- Zombuul (fake death)

---

## ⚠️ Key Rule

> **Death count alone is never enough to identify the Demon.**

---

# 🧩 Step 1 — Count Expected Deaths

Ask:

- how many deaths would the Demon cause?
- how many additional sources could apply?

---

# 🔄 Step 2 — Add Alternatives

For each death:

- could this be non-Demon?
- could this be delayed?
- could something have prevented another death?

---

# 🧠 Step 3 — Build Combinations

Instead of:

> “This caused the deaths.”

Think:

> **“Which combination explains all deaths?”**

---

## ⚠️ Key Rule

> **BMR deaths are best explained in layers, not individually.**

---

# ⚠️ Common Mistakes

### ❌ Assuming all deaths are Demon kills

Often false.

---

### ❌ Ignoring delayed effects

Some deaths originate from previous nights.

---

### ❌ Overlooking protection

Missing deaths are just as important.

---

# 🎯 Practical Heuristic

When analyzing deaths:

1. How many died?
2. How many should have died?
3. What could add deaths?
4. What could prevent deaths?
5. Could this be delayed?

---

# 🧠 Final Insight

> **In Bad Moon Rising, understanding death is not about counting bodies — it is about understanding causes.**
